Privacy Policy for Hi Zombie

Last updated: May 9, 2026

Data Collection

Hi Zombie does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information from its users. The app:

  • Does not require an account or login.
  • Does not connect to our servers (no backend).
  • Does not include any third-party tracking SDKs (no analytics, no ads).

Local Data

The following data is stored only on your device, never transmitted:

  • Game progress, settings, and unlocked content (via UserDefaults).
  • This data is automatically deleted when you uninstall the app.

Third-Party Services

The app integrates with Apple Game Center for leaderboards. Score submissions are sent to Apple's Game Center service under Apple's privacy policy. We do not control or access this data directly. See https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/ for details.

Multipeer Connectivity (Local Co-op)

Two-player local co-op uses Apple's MultipeerConnectivity framework over Bluetooth and local Wi-Fi. No data leaves the two devices participating in the local match.

Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children under 13.

Contact

隐私政策（中文）

最后更新：2026 年 5 月 9 日

数据收集

Hi Zombie 不收集、存储或传输任何用户的个人信息。本应用：

  • 无需账号或登录
  • 不连接任何后端服务器
  • 不集成任何第三方追踪 SDK（无统计、无广告）

本地数据

以下数据仅存储在你的设备本地，从不上传：

  • 游戏进度、设置、解锁内容（通过 UserDefaults 存储）
  • 这些数据会在你卸载应用时自动删除

第三方服务

本应用使用 Apple Game Center 排行榜功能。分数提交进入 Apple 的 Game Center 服务，受 Apple 隐私政策约束。详见 https://www.apple.com/cn/legal/privacy/

本地双人模式

本地双人使用 Apple MultipeerConnectivity（蓝牙 / 本地 Wi-Fi 直连），数据仅在两台设备间传输，不离开本地网络。

儿童隐私

本应用不会主动收集任何用户的数据，包括 13 岁以下儿童。
