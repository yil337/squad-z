Frequently asked questions

常见问题

How do I start local 2-player co-op? 如何开始本地双人联机？

Both players need iPhones or iPads on the same Wi-Fi network — Bluetooth also works. From the Main Menu, tap 2P. One device hosts, the other joins. Both must allow "Local Network" permission when iOS prompts.

两台 iPhone 或 iPad 在同一 Wi-Fi 下即可，蓝牙也行。主菜单点 2P，一台主机一台加入。两台都要在 iOS 询问时允许"本地网络"权限。

I bought a tip in the Tip Jar but didn't see the hearts. 我打赏后没看到爱心。

Open Community → Wall of Survivors; hearts should appear once the App Store finalizes the purchase. If they don't show within a few minutes, restart the app. If still missing, email us with your purchase receipt and we'll investigate.

打开 Community → Wall of Survivors，购买完成后爱心会显示。如果几分钟内仍未出现，请重启游戏。仍有问题请把购买收据发邮件给我们查询。

The leaderboard isn't loading. 排行榜加载不出来。

The leaderboard uses Apple Game Center, which requires an internet connection and a signed-in Game Center account. Open iOS Settings → Game Center to confirm sign-in. If still failing, try toggling Game Center off and on, then relaunch the app.

排行榜基于 Apple Game Center，需要网络且已登录 Game Center。打开 iOS 设置 → Game Center 确认已登录。仍不行可尝试切换 Game Center 后重启游戏。

I lost my run progress. 我的进度没了。

Squad Z autosaves the active run when you background the app (e.g., during a call or via the app switcher). When you reopen, "Single Player" lets you resume. Progress is intentionally wiped when you finish a run, restart, or enter a boss arena. Saves are local to the device and not synced across devices.

Squad Z 会在你切后台时自动保存当前进度，重开后点 "Single Player" 可以续局。每局结束、重开、或进入 boss 战时存档会清空 — 这是设计如此。存档仅本机，不跨设备同步。

Does the game work offline? 游戏能离线玩吗？

Yes. The full single-player game works offline. Internet is only required for Game Center leaderboards, Tip Jar purchases, and submitting suggestions via Community → Leave a Message. Local 2-player co-op uses Wi-Fi or Bluetooth between your two devices — no internet needed.

是的。完整单人游戏可离线。需要联网的功能：Game Center 排行榜、Tip Jar 打赏、Community → Leave a Message 反馈提交。本地双人联机走 Wi-Fi 或蓝牙直连，不需要外网。

Why does the game ask for "Local Network" permission? 为什么要"本地网络"权限？

Only for local 2-player co-op. The game uses Apple's MultipeerConnectivity framework, which discovers nearby devices via Bonjour on the local network. Squad Z does not transmit any data outside your local network — no tracking, no analytics, no external servers.

仅本地双人联机需要。游戏使用 Apple 的 MultipeerConnectivity 框架，通过本地 Bonjour 发现附近设备。Squad Z 不向本地网络外传输任何数据 — 无追踪、无分析、无外部服务器。

How do I report a bug? 如何报告 bug？

Email yidong289@gmail.com with a brief description of the bug, your device model (e.g., "iPhone 14 Pro"), your iOS version (Settings → General → About → iOS Version), and your game version (Main Menu → Settings → Version). Screenshots or screen recordings help a lot.

邮件 yidong289@gmail.com，附上 bug 简述、设备型号（如 "iPhone 14 Pro"）、iOS 版本（设置 → 通用 → 关于本机）、游戏版本（主菜单 → 设置 → 版本）。截图或屏幕录像帮助很大。

I want a refund. 我想退款。

Refunds for App Store purchases — including Tip Jar tips — are handled by Apple, not by the developer. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com and sign in with your Apple ID to submit a refund request.

App Store 购买（包括 Tip Jar 打赏）的退款由 Apple 处理，不通过开发者。访问 reportaproblem.apple.com 用你的 Apple ID 登录提交退款申请。

The game crashes or freezes. 游戏闪退 / 卡死。

First try force-quitting the app, then relaunch. If the issue persists, restart your device. If still happening, email us with: the moment it crashes (e.g., "during boss fight"), device model, iOS version, and game version. We track all reported crashes and ship fixes in following updates.

先试强制退出 app 后重开。仍有问题可重启设备。还不行就邮件告诉我们：闪退的场景（如"打 boss 时"）、设备型号、iOS 版本、游戏版本。我们会跟踪所有上报的闪退并在后续更新中修复。

Privacy Policy

隐私政策

What we collect

数据收集

Squad Z (Hi Zombie) does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information from its users. The app requires no account creation, sign-up, or sign-in to play.

Squad Z (Hi Zombie) 不收集、存储或传输任何用户的个人信息。游玩本游戏无需创建账号、注册或登录。

Local data

本地数据

Game progress, settings, and preferences are stored locally on your device using Apple's UserDefaults system. This data never leaves your device. When you uninstall the app, all locally stored data is automatically deleted by iOS.

游戏进度、设置和偏好通过 Apple UserDefaults 存储在本机。这些数据不会离开你的设备。卸载 app 时，所有本地数据会被 iOS 自动删除。

Apple Game Center

Apple Game Center（排行榜）

The optional global leaderboard uses Apple's Game Center, which displays your Game Center alias alongside your top scores (distance traveled, zombies killed). Game Center data is handled by Apple — see Apple's Privacy Policy. You can disable score uploading in Settings → Upload Scores, or sign out of Game Center entirely from iOS Settings.

全球排行榜（可选）使用 Apple Game Center，展示你的 Game Center 昵称和最高分（距离、击杀）。Game Center 数据由 Apple 管理，请参考 Apple 隐私政策。你可在 设置 → Upload Scores 关闭分数上传，或在 iOS 设置里登出 Game Center。

Local 2-player co-op

本地双人联机

Local co-op uses Apple's MultipeerConnectivity framework over Wi-Fi or Bluetooth to connect two devices directly. No game state, account information, or device identifier is transmitted to any external server during a co-op session — all communication is peer-to-peer between the two connected devices on your local network.

本地双人联机使用 Apple 的 MultipeerConnectivity 框架，通过 Wi-Fi 或蓝牙直连两台设备。联机过程中游戏状态、账号信息、设备标识符均不向任何外部服务器传输 — 所有通信只在本地网络内的两台设备之间进行。

In-app purchases (Tip Jar)

内购（打赏）

The optional Tip Jar uses Apple's StoreKit framework. Purchase transactions are handled entirely by Apple — Squad Z does not see, store, or transmit any payment information. We only record an anonymous count of total "hearts" earned, stored locally on your device.

可选的 Tip Jar 通过 Apple StoreKit 处理。所有交易完全由 Apple 处理 — Squad Z 不接触、存储或传输任何付款信息。我们仅在本地记录累计获得的爱心总数。

iCloud sync (Tip Jar hearts only)

iCloud 同步（仅 Tip Jar 爱心）

The total number of "hearts" you've accumulated through the Tip Jar is synchronized across your devices using Apple's iCloud Key-Value Store. This is the only data we sync, and it never leaves Apple's iCloud infrastructure.

通过 Tip Jar 积累的爱心总数通过 Apple iCloud Key-Value Store 在你的设备间同步。这是我们唯一同步的数据，且仅存留于 Apple iCloud 基础设施内。

Community suggestions

建议箱（CloudKit）

If you choose to submit feedback through Community → Leave a Message, your message is sent through Apple's CloudKit to a private database accessible only to the developer. Submissions are tied to your iCloud account but are not made public. We may use submissions to improve the game.

如果你通过 Community → Leave a Message 提交反馈，消息会通过 Apple CloudKit 发送到仅开发者可访问的私有数据库。提交内容与你的 iCloud 账户关联，但不会公开。我们可能根据反馈改进游戏。

Third-party tracking

第三方追踪

Squad Z does not use any third-party tracking, analytics, advertising SDKs, or fingerprinting. There are no ads.

Squad Z 不使用任何第三方追踪、数据分析、广告 SDK 或指纹识别。游戏内无广告。

Children's privacy

儿童隐私

Squad Z is rated 12+ on the App Store. We do not knowingly collect personal information from any user, including minors. If you believe a minor has provided personal information through Community → Leave a Message, please email us and we will delete it.

Squad Z 在 App Store 上的年龄分级为 12+。我们不会有意收集任何用户的个人信息，包括未成年人。如果你认为未成年人通过 Community → Leave a Message 提交了个人信息，请发邮件给我们，我们将删除。

Changes to this policy

政策变更

If we update this policy, the change will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date at the bottom. For material changes, we may also note them in the App Store "What's New" section of the next release.

如更新本政策，本页底部的"Last updated"日期会同步更新。重大变更也可能在下一版本 App Store 的"What's New"中说明。

About

关于

Squad Z (Hi Zombie) is a pixel-art zombie survival runner for iOS, built by one developer over a year of late nights. Free, no ads, no paywalls. Solo or local 2-player couch co-op.

Squad Z (Hi Zombie) 是一款像素风末日生存跑酷 iOS 游戏，由一名开发者用一年的时间独立完成。免费，无广告，无付费墙。支持单人或本地双人沙发联机。

Built with Swift & SpriteKit. Currently in TestFlight, launching on the App Store soon.

用 Swift 和 SpriteKit 开发，目前 TestFlight 公测中，即将正式上架 App Store。
